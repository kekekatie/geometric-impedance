analyses in this repository were carried out in close collaboration with

The collaboration was genuine and iterative: the human author held the research
question, the physical intuition, and the standards of evidence; the AI
collaborators contributed derivations, wrote and ran the substrate/holonomy code,
proposed and stress-tested constructions, caught their own errors, and helped
enforce the pre-registration discipline (sealing predictions before building the
constructions that tested them). Failed and null constructions were retained and
reported alongside the positive results, per the programme's house rules.

Per-commit co-authorship is recorded in the git history via `Co-Authored-By`
trailers. The sealed pre-registrations, timestamped commits, and frozen release
(`v1.0-paper`) constitute the auditable record of who did what, when.
