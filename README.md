# Geometric Impedance

Research papers exploring geometric structure in physics and AI systems.

## Toy Model Papers

Investigations into how aperiodic geometry (Penrose tilings) channels and preserves patterns differently than periodic or random substrates.

| Paper | Key Finding |
|-------|-------------|
| [Pattern Persistence and Geometric Channelling](toy_model/Pattern_Persistence_Geometric_Channelling.pdf) | Aperiodic substrates channel patterns back toward origin better than periodic or random |
| [Connectivity, Memory, and Persistence](toy_model/Connectivity_Memory_Persistence.pdf) | The geometry IS the memory - native tile-edge connectivity provides 20:1 memory benefit |

## AI Minds

Three papers examining geometric and structural properties of AI cognition.

| Paper | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| [Geometric Order Emerging in LLMs](ai_minds/Geometric_Order_Emerging_in_LLMs.pdf) | Synthesis of mechanistic interpretability research showing geometric structure emerging in transformers |
| [What the Persona Selection Model Cannot See](ai_minds/Persona_Selection_Model_Critique.pdf) | Critique of Anthropic's PSM paper; proposes crystallisation vs selection |
| [The Breakstep Principle](ai_minds/Epilepsy_Breakstep_How_Minds_Form.pdf) | How minds must form - constraints from epilepsy research and the breakstep principle |

---

*These papers explore a common thread: that geometry isn't just where things happen - it's how they persist, channel, and remember.*
