## Key question: does Penrose naturally pass through alpha~0.553?

**AB_N30** never drops below α=0.553 in the tested range.
**Penrose_N24** never drops below α=0.553 in the tested range.

## Interpretation rules

- This does NOT prove Berry curvature
- This does NOT prove the tiling 'has' alpha=0.553
- It tests whether a depth-resistance walk produces a natural crossing/plateau
- The key evidence would be: Penrose crosses 0.553 with a flatter slope (plateau)
  while AB crosses at a different lambda or with a steeper slope
- If both substrates produce identical curves, the effect is generic to the walk model, not the substrate
