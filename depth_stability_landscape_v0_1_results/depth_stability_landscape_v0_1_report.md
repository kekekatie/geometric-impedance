## Interpretation rules

- NOT Berry curvature. NOT quantum holonomy. NOT alpha~0.553.
- No walks involved — this is pure tiling geometry.
- If depth predicts neighbour mismatch: the stability landscape is a
  static geometric fact, not a dynamical walk effect.
- The controlled linger experiment (v0.1) showed address residue is
  instantaneous and positional. This test maps that landscape directly.
