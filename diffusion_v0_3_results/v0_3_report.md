## Key comparisons

## Interpretation

This is a classical address-transport proxy. It does not claim Berry curvature, does not claim alpha~0.553, and should be read as testing whether texture-rich regions statistically produce more address residue on near-return paths.

The key question: is the texture high-low gap stable across seeds, and does Penrose show a stronger or more stable effect than AB? If the gap disappears under shuffled controls or is inconsistent across seeds, the effect is not robust.
